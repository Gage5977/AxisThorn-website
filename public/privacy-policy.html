1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)

  • Financial data shared for analysis and consulting services
  • Payment information processed through secure third-party providers

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered financial consulting services
  • Process payments and manage client accounts
  • Communicate about services and provide support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in our operations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Secure data processing environments

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Port your data to another service provider

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide ongoing services to active clients
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Financial data is typically retained for 7 years in accordance with standard accounting practices and regulatory requirements.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.
